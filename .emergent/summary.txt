**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
*   **AI Digital Twin (TWINNEE):** An AI chatbot that acts as a friendly companion.

    *   It gently tracks behavior patterns (screen time, learning, creativity, mood) without surveillance.

*   **Social Collaboration Platform:** A networking feature for kids.

    *   The TWINNEE AI acts as a mediator and friend during collaboration, analyzing the interaction and generating reports for parents.
*   **Parent Dashboard:** A PIN-protected area for parents to view behavior scores, interaction reports, and manage settings.
*   **Landing Page:** A new, modern landing page reflecting the TWINNEE brand and features.
*   **Video Editor:** A timeline-based video editor to assemble scenes, add audio, and export videos, enhanced with a drag-and-drop media library.
